About Me

Mechanical Engineer turned Web Developer

My Story

Hey there. My name is Stephen Doty. I am a Full Stack Web Developer that will be graduating from the DigitalCrafts Full-Time Web Development Bootcamp in January 2022. I graduated from college with a Mechanical Engineering degree and after 7 years of working in that industry, I decided to look into the tech world and I'm glad I did.

I have come to really enjoy computer programming and how starting from almost nothing, you can take an idea, break it down, and then using code and problem solving, build something amazing.

My Interests

I enjoy all things tech and coding related. I also enjoy playing video games and my guitars, both acoustic and electric.
