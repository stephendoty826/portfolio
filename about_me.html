About Me

Mechanical Engineer turned Software Developer

My Story

Hi, I'm Stephen Doty, a Full Stack Web Developer with a strong foundation in engineering and problem-solving. After earning my degree in Mechanical Engineering and spending 7 years in the industry, I made the transition into tech and graduated from the DigitalCrafts Full-Time Web Development Bootcamp in January 2022.

Since then, I’ve focused on building modern, responsive web applications using technologies like JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, and Python. I’m experienced in front-end development with tools like Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap, and React-Bootstrap, and I enjoy creating clean, functional user interfaces. On the backend, I’ve worked with relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL), Sequelize, and RESTful API design, and I’m continuing to deepen my skills in building scalable server-side applications.

One of my current projects is the Helldivers 2 Loadout Vault — a React-based web app where players can create, filter, and randomize custom loadouts for Helldivers 2. It features dynamic component rendering, persistent state with local storage, and a drag-and-drop interface for organizing saved loadouts. I'm actively developing the backend to enable user accounts and cloud-based data persistence.

I love the challenge of taking an idea from concept to code, breaking down complex problems, and building practical tools with a great user experience. Whether working independently or collaborating in a team, I bring curiosity, clarity, and a strong technical mindset to every project.

My Interests

I have a strong passion for technology and all things related to coding, from building web applications to exploring new tools and frameworks. In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, which often inspire my interest in game design and interactive experiences. I'm also a musician at heart—I love playing both acoustic and electric guitar, and I find that music, much like coding, blends creativity with technical skill in a deeply satisfying way.
